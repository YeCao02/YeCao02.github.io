### [Influencing factors and planning strategies for street spatial quality from dual perspectives: A case study of Shenzhen's Nanshan-Bao'an area](https://doi.org/10.26914/c.cnkihy.2025.047913)

Yudong Lin, **Ye Cao**, and Zongcai Wei

*Proceedings of the 2025 China Urban Planning Annual Conference, 2025*

Co-first author. Constructed a street-quality dataset by sampling street-view images, designing paired-comparison questionnaires for 273 residents, and combining semantic segmentation, ArcGIS Pro, and machine-learning-based label extraction to identify built-environment drivers of street quality in Shenzhen.

### Can multimodal large language models approximate children's urban perceptions? Evaluating Qwen3-VL for child-friendly city assessment

**Ye Cao**, Yuxin Gao, and Sophie Hadfield-Hill*

*Submitted to Computers, Environment and Urban Systems*

Evaluates whether multimodal large language models can approximate children's urban perceptions of child-friendly streetscapes in Shenzhen using ground-truth ratings from 158 children across 220 child-centered street-view images. The study compares locally deployed Qwen3-VL variants across scale, decoding regime, prompt language, and persona framing, and combines agreement metrics, semantic analysis, and t-SNE visualization.
